About TheAngel

Democratizing startup investment globally

Our Mission

We believe the best startup investments shouldn't be limited to a privileged few. TheAngel is building a global platform that connects ambitious founders with passionate investors, breaking down barriers to capital.

By tokenizing equity and enabling peer-to-peer investment, we're creating a more accessible, transparent, and efficient way to fund the next generation of world-changing companies.
