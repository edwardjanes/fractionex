Welcome to TheAngel

Getting Started

TheAngel is a global platform that connects investors with founders. Whether you're an investor looking for opportunities or a founder seeking capital, you're in the right place.

This documentation will guide you through:

  • Creating and verifying your account
  • Making your first investment
  • Listing your startup for fundraising
  • Trading equity tokens
  • Managing your portfolio

Account Setup

Step 1: Create Your Account

Click the "Sign Up" button and choose your user type (Investor or Founder). Enter your email and create a password.

Step 2: Verify Your Email

Check your email for a verification link and click it to confirm your account.

Step 3: Complete KYC

Provide your basic information and complete our Know-Your-Customer verification process. This typically takes 5-10 minutes.

Step 4: Fund Your Account

Add funds via bank transfer, credit card, or cryptocurrency. Your funds will be available immediately after verification.

Investor Guide

Ready to invest in startups? Here's how to get started:

Browsing Opportunities

Visit the "Explore" section to view available investment opportunities. Filter by industry, location, or stage.

Making an Investment

Click on a startup to view details, financials, and the founding team. Click "Invest" to proceed with your investment.

Receiving Tokens

Your equity tokens will be credited to your wallet immediately after the investment is confirmed.

Portfolio Management

View all your investments in your dashboard. Track valuations, updates, and milestones from each company.

Founder Fundraising Guide

Ready to raise capital? Follow these steps:

Apply to List

Submit your company profile, financials, and pitch deck through our application portal.

Due Diligence

Our team will review your application. We may request additional documents or information.

Listing Approval

Once approved, we'll prepare your fundraising page and market materials.

Launch Campaign

Your listing goes live. Start receiving investments and grow your cap table.

Trading & Liquidity

One of the unique features of TheAngel is the ability to trade your equity tokens on our secondary market.

Selling Your Tokens

List your tokens on the marketplace at your desired price. Other investors can purchase them from you.

Price Discovery

Token prices are determined by supply and demand. Check the market data to understand current valuations.

Trading History

View your trading history and track your gains/losses in real-time through your dashboard.

Security & Compliance

Your Security

We use industry-leading security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Cold storage for digital assets
  • Regular security audits

Regulatory Compliance

TheAngel is fully compliant with securities regulations in all jurisdictions where we operate. All investments are conducted under proper legal frameworks.

Best Practices

Always enable 2FA, never share your password, and verify URLs before logging in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What's the minimum investment?

A: You can invest from as little as $100, making startup investing accessible to everyone.

Q: How long does verification take?

A: Most verifications complete within 5-10 minutes. Complex cases may take up to 24 hours.

Q: Can I withdraw my investment?

A: You can trade your tokens on our marketplace. Liquidity depends on market demand.

Q: Are there fees?

A: We charge a 5% success fee on investments and a 2% fee on token trades. Transparent pricing, no hidden fees.

Q: What countries do you operate in?

A: We currently support investors and founders from 100+ countries. Check our platform for your region's availability.
